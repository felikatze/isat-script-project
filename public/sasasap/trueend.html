True End

Compilation page for scenes relating to START AGAIN's True End.


This ending is obtained by getting 10+ Weird Points by the time you enter the last hallway. There are a wide variety of actions that award Weird Points, so meeting this requirement isn't hard as long as you make sure to be as weird as possible.


Some weird actions include:

  • Using "(Just attack)" 4+ times before entering the hallway before the infirmary
  • Being totally fine after getting the Perfect Ending twice
  • Picking dialogue options that "miss your cue" to make puns or just act in general (Such as saying "Blood on your hands!" to the orange poem, or missing the yeast pun in the storage room)
  • Picking up keys before you've even found the door they're for (including using the torch before ever interacting with the hair wall)
  • Zone out of conversations 3 times
  • Walk down the wrong path and turn around 3 or 5 times
  • Don't pray when interacting with the intact Change God statue
