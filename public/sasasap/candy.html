Candy Scene / Last Hallway

The candy scene for the True End of START AGAIN.

This is the only non-generic text in this hallway.


This scene is triggered by having 10 or more Weird Points when passing through the hallway. Weird Points are awarded for generally, well, weird actions, such as picking up keys before interacting with their doors, missing cues in social interactions, skipping conversations, walking down wrong hallways, etc.

Unlike ISAT, START AGAIN's weird points don't have a visual/auditory cue, but you can tell something was weird when everybody looks at Siffrin funny, generally.
