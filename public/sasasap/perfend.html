Perfect End

Compilation page for scenes relating to START AGAIN's "perfect" ending.

This ending is obtained by getting less than 4 Weird Points. This is mainly done through choosing appropriate dialogue options and completing goals in order (i.e. first seeing a locked door, then finding the key for it, instead of the other way around).
