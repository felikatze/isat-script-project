King Questions

Created and reformatted by felikatze

All questions you can ask the King in ACT 3 and 4, including variations for quest progress and Acts.

All questions, excluding "What's Wish Craft?" and "Flower for you." are not available after completing Kingquest.

For the finale of Kingquest, see "Let's stop fighting."

See also: Kingquest.
