Left side Hallways


Smiling Key Door

Source: Map071 (Hallway (Locked 3-1)), Map177 (ACT5_Hallway (Locked 3-1))

The hallway immediately to the left of the central room. Leads to the pottery room (page WIP), the hallway leading to the Observatory, and the hallway with three rooms.

In ACT 5, there's a ghost in here that plays no text.

Without the key

Interaction text is generic. The key is the Smiling Key ("shaped like a smiling mask"), found in the room with the mirror on the right.

With the key

Interaction text is generic.


Stairs to the Observatory

Source: Map093 (StairstoSTARS (Tears)), Map178(ACT5_StairstoSTARS (Tears))

The hallway above the hallway with the Smiling Key door. It has a row of tears, which play generic text.

In ACT 5, this hallway is (usually) in the same place, has some tears which play equally generic text, and a ghost which plays no text.

Ghost

Source: Map108 (HallwayToKey (Ghost3)), Map189 (ACT5_HallwayToKey(Ghost3))

Past the bathroom, there's a ghost. Usually, there's no text, but the event plays here if you saw all three ghosts in one loop; see the main Memory of Ghosts page.

In ACT 5, there's a cluster of five ghosts, which always appear and play no text.

Angry Key Door

Source: Map092 (Hallway (Locked 3-2))

The hallway immediately to the right of the central room. Leads the to the room with the crest, which is locked, and the hallway to the Mirror Room and Writing Room.
