Talking to her first

Dialogue when talking to Euphrasie before you've finished talking to everybody else. You only get new dialogue the first time you try to talk to her in any given loop.

Subsequent times in the same loop, she boots straight to generic dialogue. TLDR: it takes five loops to get all of this dialogue.

There are portrait changes for repeating these, implying they used to all play in sequence. They don't, so these changes go unused.


Start

This dialogue always occurs, no matter how often it's been. At the end of this is when Siffrin's responses change for new dialogue. Imagine this bit in front of all the other dialogues.

The very first time you talk to Euphrasie, there's no additional dialogue after this.
