Left side hallways


Egg Key Door

Source: Map075 (Hallway (Locked 1-1))

The hallway immediately to the left of the central room. Leads to the armory and the hallway with the ghost, which is locked.

Without the key

Interaction text is generic. The key is the Egg Key, found in the kitchen on the right.

With the key

Interaction text is generic.


Ghost

Source: Map125 (Hallway (Ghost1))

Past the egg key locked door, there's a ghost. There's no text for this one, though.
