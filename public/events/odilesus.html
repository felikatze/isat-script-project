Odile Sus Events

Dialogue compiled by memeseller (tumblr baketothefuture)

Acting weird enough for Odile to both notice, and also finally talk about.

To unlock, read the Time Craft book in the Candle-making room as well as the books on Wish Craft in the locked Storage Room on Floor 1 and the Secret Library on Floor 2.

Additionally, the player needs to obtain a total of 14 Weird points and talk to Odile during the third Snack Break.

The additional scene in the third snack break does not occur on a loop with all Friendquests completed. The actual event itself still does, though.

For more details on Weird points, check out the respective wiki page on the event

Source: Common Event 132 (OdileSidequest)
