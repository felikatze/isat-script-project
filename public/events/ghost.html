Ghost Event

Source: Map108 (HallwayToKey(Ghost3))

All text surrounding ghosts and the Ghost Event. It occurs when all three ghosts are encountered in one consecutive loop (savescumming possible) and then entering the long hallway before obtaining the KeyKnife, where the third and last ghost is located.


Ghosts have a 1 in 15 chance of appearing in ACT 2, 1 in 5 in ACT 3, and 1 in 3 in ACT 4.

For more details, check out the wiki page.
