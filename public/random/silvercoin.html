Your coin. There's only one like it in the Universe.



Usage

The usage text when using the Silver Coin is decided by RNG. The game rolls a number between 1 and 51. "The Incident" triggers at 61 or higher.

Since rolling a 61 naturally is impossible, several factors add to the rolled RNG.

In addition to making the Incident monologue possible to reach, the additions also make the lower RNG options less likely or even impossible to occur. This also means viewing the Incident is not possible in ACT 2, as the amount of bonuses available does not amount to +10.

Of note: additions to the Silver Coin Help only occur the first time each condition is met.


Factors include:

  • Showing Loop the coin in ACT 2 & 3: +1 each
  • Showing Loop the coin in ACT 4: +5
  • Interact with the croissants in the Boulangerie in ACT 3 or 4: +3
  • Interact with the croissants in the Boulangerie with Odile during her Friendquest: +3
  • Interact with the croissants in the locked Break Room on Floor 2: +1
  • Interact with the open book in the room with the dog on Floor 3: +1
  • Read the book on Wish Craft in the Secret Library: +1
  • Use the coin in ACT 3: +10 (does not stack with ACT 4 & book bonuses)
  • Use the coin in ACT 4: +15 (does not stack with ACT 3 & book bonuses)
  • Use the coin after reading the first book on Wish Craft in the locked Storage Room +20 (does not stack with ACT 3 & 4 bonuses)
